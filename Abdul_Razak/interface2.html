Lung Cancer Risk Distribution

This chart shows how patients are distributed across Low, Medium, and High lung cancer risk levels. It gives the main overview before exploring symptoms in more detail.

Symptom Severity Breakdown

This pie chart shows the severity distribution of the selected symptom. When a risk level is selected from the bar chart, the pie chart updates to show that group only.

Pie colours represent symptom severity: light blue = low, medium blue = medium, dark blue = high.
