Dashboard Overview

This dashboard shows how survival changes across different stages of breast cancer.

It compares outcomes between chemotherapy and non-chemotherapy groups. Lower survival in chemotherapy cases does not indicate ineffective treatment, but reflects that these patients are typically in more advanced stages.

Key Insight: Survival decreases as cancer stage increases, highlighting the importance of early detection and timely treatment.
